About Us


Tasha's restaurant serves traditional Spanish tapas dishes.
The Spanish practice of going out for tapas, also called el tapeo had
its humble beginnings long ago with the first
recipes being written in the 14th century!

Tasha's is a family owned and run resturant in the heart
of Cape Town's V&A Waterfront. We source local produce from our famous Waterfront market,
guaranteeing fresh ingredients at the highest quality. With magical views and friendly service, why not come join our
table and share in the vibe that is tapas!
